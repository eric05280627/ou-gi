# ou-gi《橫議》
To anyone who happens upon these writings:

"ou-gi" is a philosophical work that appears in the world of my story "Nihonki". It compiles ideas from the fields of existence, ethics, and politics, and offers reflections on modern civilization. Like "Nihonki," this is written in classical Chinese.

If you do not find my humble thoughts and crude and difficult-to-read language offensive, and if it can have a positive impact on you, I will be very grateful.
